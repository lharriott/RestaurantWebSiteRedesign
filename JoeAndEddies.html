About Us

Joe & Eddies Family Restaurant serves fresh, Southern home cooking. Locally owned since 1954, Joe & Eddies is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We have free WiFi and serve beer and wine. Start your day in Fort Walton Beach with a hearty breakfast at our family restaurant by sampling our World Famous Omelettes or our Eggs, Bacon, and Ham or Belgian Waffles topped with Blueberries. Stop by for lunch or dinner and browse through our menu for our Weekly Specials or our Burgers, Deli Style Sandwiches served on freshly baked french or wheat rolls, Country Fried Steak, fried or grilled Catfish, Rib Eye Steak, Garden Salads, and more. Don't forget to leave room for our delicious homemade desserts. Kids can choose from our children's menu. Joe & Eddies is Southern Home cooking at its finest.
